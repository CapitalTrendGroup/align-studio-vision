Structured Growth

The Referral Engine

Referrals are the highest quality, lowest cost source of new clients. But they must be engineered, not hoped for.

1. Be Referable

Deliver an experience so good that NOT talking about it would be strange. The feeling is the prerequisite.

2. Give Them the Words

The Talking Logo: "Only four per class, they know your name, the science behind every movement." Easy to repeat.

3. Identify Champions

Top 20% most enthusiastic members. Founding Member status. Early access. Direct line to Katie.

4. Create Triggers

Ask after milestones (10th class), after positive feedback, at the Day 60 check-in. Never cold-ask at every class.

5. Reward Both Sides

Referrer: free class credit. Friend: intro offer. Both must benefit for the system to sustain.

6. Track Everything

App referral codes. Monthly rate. "How did you hear about us?" on every intake form. Data drives decisions.
