Dear Katie,

We read every word of your questionnaire. Not as a form — as a portrait of someone about to do something brave.

When you told us your biggest fear is "getting clients," we want you to know something: that fear is universal among founders, and it is almost always unfounded for people who think the way you do.

You are not someone who trained in Pilates and decided to open a studio. You are someone with a first-class degree in Business Management and Marketing who fell in love with Pilates and recognised a gap in the market. That is a fundamentally different starting position.

Your instinct to build a walk and talk club. Your vision of Pilates and coffee mornings. Your insistence on education and the science behind Pilates. Your gender-neutral branding designed to welcome men. Your idea to film local rugby players trying reformer Pilates. These are not the ideas of someone who will struggle to find clients.

You described your ideal studio as "calm and serene — a place for workout but also to take a second to close your eyes and reconnect with your breath." You want clients to leave feeling "refreshed, yet challenged." You want to be known as "a community, committed to wellness and education."

These are not vague aspirations. They are a brand positioning so clear that a strategy consultant would charge thousands to arrive at the same conclusion. You already know, intuitively, what needs to be built.

The evidence says you are going to need a waitlist. Let us show you how.

Reframing

The Micro-Studio Advantage

Four reformers is not a limitation. It is your greatest brand asset.

Personalisation

Katie knows every client by name. Their goals. Their injuries. Their lives. A 12-reformer studio cannot offer this.

Exclusivity

"Only 4 spots per class" creates natural urgency. No hard selling needed — scarcity does the work.

Quality

With 4 clients, every person gets attention. Form corrections happen in real time. Results come faster.

Premium Positioning

Forma Pilates in LA charges $75+/session with 3–4 clients. Micro-studio is the luxury segment of Pilates.

When ALIGN. has a waitlist (and it will), that waitlist becomes your most powerful marketing tool. "There's a 2-week wait for a spot at ALIGN." is a message money cannot buy.
