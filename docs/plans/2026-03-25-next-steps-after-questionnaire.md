This is the document Eduardo needs to begin building:
- Detailed feature specifications
- Screen-by-screen wireframes
- User journey maps
- Payment integration details (Stripe)

Eduardo mentioned a mockup in two to three days and the full app in approximately two weeks — the specification will make that timeline achievable.
